Overview

       An action adventure, fighting, Progression, Fantasy type single player RPG 10+ game. In game music will be based on reggae, ragga and soca-type beats. Player starts game as a 15 year old child on a Transport Board Bus coming into Bridgetown for the first time. After disembarking, the player will then immediately meet a woman named Mudda Sally, who will be the player's guide in the early game. Player will be allowed to explore bridgetown before entering a rum shop which contains an Old Man seemingly having the time of his life drinking at the bar who calls the player over to him. This old man, as Mudda Sally will explain, is the Barbadian Guru of Wonders whose age is unknown and will be the main source of game progression.

When speaking with the old man, the old man will reveal that he is also a story teller. He will then invite the player to have a seat and listen to a story of his past.

Unable to say no, The old man will then begin to tell his tales.
Each story will be in the form of an interactive game where the player will play as the "Old man" and be able to explore various timelines based on what story the old man is telling. Tales will involve historical events such as The Spanish Invasion, Bussa Revolution, House of Assembly etc. After each story, the player will be awared experience which contributes to a leveling system, and some form of currency which they can use to purchase items which they can use when they go exploring outside of the old man's tales.

As the player levels up, new areas will become accessible to them.
The old man's tales will also contain interactions with Popular folklore such as Anansi the Spider, Shaggy Bear, Ossi More etc. Outside of the Old Man's tales,the old man will send the player on quests for items which will require the player to explore popular areas such as Harrison Cave and Gun hill Station and other random areas like forests where they will encounter enemies like Green Monkeys and fight Bosses like Bull Cow, Mother Hen and Bumblebee, and also get recovery items like Mangoes and Tamarinds.

Eventually, After listening to all of the old man's stories, the old man will eventually pass away grateful to you for listening to his tales and being there with him. By this time, the player will have aged significantly, and will eventually resign and take the Old Man's Place as a story teller. A cutscene will then play of the player interacting with another young child, and eventually passing away at which point the game will end.
